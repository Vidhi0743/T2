Clue 3: Fibonacci sequence leads to a hidden word.

The Fibonacci sequence holds a secret when applied to the alphabet. Use the first few numbers in the sequence and apply an offset shift to decode the word.

Fibonacci Sequence: 1, 16, 6, 26, 2, 25

For each Fibonacci number, match it to its corresponding letter in the alphabet. However, each letter is then offset by a certain amount. The shift pattern is +1, +2, +3, etc.

The first few numbers give you the following letters (applying the Fibonacci sequence and the shifts):

  • 1 → A, Shift by +1 → B
  • 1 → A, Shift by +2 → C
  • 2 → B, Shift by +3 → E
  • 3 → C, Shift by +4 → G
  • 5 → E, Shift by +5 → J
  • 8 → H, Shift by +6 → N

After applying this pattern, what word do you get?

Hint: The word is a key concept often seen in Da Vinci's work.

Correct! The word is *BRIDGE*. Da Vinci sketched designs for bridges in his notebooks!
